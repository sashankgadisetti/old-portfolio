Hi, My name is
Sashank Gadisetti

I am currently a junior at Dublin High School and have been in love with the biomedical sciences ever since freshman year.

One of my strongest beliefs is that there is no substitute for hard work. I am a goal oriented person, meaning that I like to set a clear path for myself and give my goals 100% of my effort.

Of the many lessons school has taught me, one of the most important lessons is that being good at something and liking something are two very different things. Science, at least for me, is a challenging subject which requires one to think and come to conclusion, almost always which may have to be reconsidered. It requires a constant cycle of thinking and experimentation, each time bringing new information and assumptions to light. It was these challenges and ability to gain a deeper understanding of a subject caused me to like it even more, and ultimately gain my interest in the medical field.
